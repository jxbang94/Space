Underfunded R&D became 1st to land rover on an unexplored part of the moon!

India became the fourth nation to land successfully on the moon and the first to land and deploy a rover in the southern polar region, an area of keen scientific interest.

With this moon landing, it opens up a possibility for eventually a habitat of some sort, and is something that is very attractive to expaning human life outside of Earth.

Japan launches moon lander and X-ray observatory

Japan sent a new X-ray space telescope into low Earth orbit and a first, small lunar lander on its way to the moon with a single launch.

James Webb Space Telescope

The Webb was launched on 25 December 2021 on an Ariane 5 rocket from Kourou, French Guiana, and arrived at the Sun Earth L2 Lagrange point in January 2022. The first Webb image was released to the public via a press conference on 11 July 2022.
